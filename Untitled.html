Our Story

Welcome to Hotel Delfin Playa, one of Playa del Carmen's oldest hotels. Here, timeless heritage meets modern Tulum style. Our family-run boutique hotel blends our rich history with contemporary comforts, ensuring a unique and memorable experience.

Our journey is captured through a collection of vintage photographs alongside chic, modern designs inspired by the natural beauty of the region. Enjoy a stay steeped in tradition with the amenities of today.
